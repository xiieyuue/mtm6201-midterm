ABOUT US

Create the most beautiful experience for people

At Flora, we're passionate about creating the most beautiful experience for people through the power of flowers. Since our founding, we've been committed to providing our customers with the highest quality blooms, exceptional customer service, and a personalized touch that sets us apart.

We believe that flowers have the ability to transform any space and brighten up any day. That's why we work tirelessly to source the freshest and most vibrant flowers from local growers and around the world. Our team of expert florists carefully handcraft each arrangement to ensure that every petal is perfectly placed.

From ours to yours

At Flora, we're more than just a flower shop. We're a community of passionate flower lovers who are dedicated to creating unforgettable moments for our customers. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or just want to brighten up someone's day, we're here to help you create the most beautiful experience possible with flowers.

OUR STORY

a story begin with a boy's hope

At Flora, our story began with a boy's hope. Our founder, Alice, grew up in a small village in the countryside, surrounded by fields of wildflowers. As a child, he dreamed of sharing the beauty of these flowers with the world.

Years later, Alice moved to the city and began working in the floral industry. But he always felt that something was missing. He wanted to create a flower shop that was more than just a place to buy flowers. He wanted to create a space that was infused with the same hope and beauty that he felt as a child.

And so, Flora was born. From our humble beginnings as a small shop in the city, we've grown into a community of passionate flower lovers who are dedicated to sharing the beauty of flowers with the world.
